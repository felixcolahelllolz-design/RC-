A Legacy of Trust Built Across Decades and Distances

Proudly Puerto Rican. Proven Across America.

Since 1997, RC Paint & Roofing has been synonymous with reliability. Founded by native Puerto Ricans, our commitment to quality craftsmanship is in our DNA. This dedication has earned us the trust of clients not only across our home island but has also taken our expertise to demanding projects throughout the United States—from the sunny coasts of Florida to the challenging seasons of Buffalo, New York, and beyond.

With over 25 years of hands-on experience, we have a proven track record with a diverse range of clients, including government agencies, schools, and homeowners. We bring the same high standard of professionalism to every job, ensuring your project is completed on time, within budget, and to your complete satisfaction.
